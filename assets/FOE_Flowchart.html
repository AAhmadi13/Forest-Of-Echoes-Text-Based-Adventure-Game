üéØ ALL POSSIBLE ENDINGS
üëë ASCENSION OF FLAMEBEARER
Path: Absorb Heart ‚Üí Fight Dragon ‚Üí Perfect Sky Battle
With the Heart's lightning power coursing through you, you leap into the sky to match the dragon in aerial combat. Your Enchanted Sword, now crackling with divine energy, pierces Pyros's heart as you both plummet toward the mountain. But your fiery aura protects you from the fall. You survive, transformed into a legendary Flamebearer - part human, part divine force. The Crown of Pyros becomes yours by right of conquest, and you ascend to become a mythical guardian of the realm.
üëª MASTER OF THE HEART
Path: Absorb Heart ‚Üí Fight Dragon ‚Üí Use Spirit Magic
The Heart of Yurei reveals its true nature - ancient spirit magic. You summon ethereal chains that bind the mighty dragon, forcing it from the sky through supernatural power. Pyros crashes to the mountain, defeated not by blade or fire, but by the ghostly magic of ages past. You claim the Crown as the new Master of the Heart, wielding both the crown's authority and Yurei's spectral powers. The spirits of the forest now serve you.
üèÜ DRAGON SLAYER
Path: Store Heart ‚Üí Fight Dragon ‚Üí Perfect Teamwork
Without supernatural powers, you rely on pure skill and friendship. Through flawless coordination with your traveler companion, you execute a perfect battle strategy. At the crucial moment, you signal your ally who distracts Pyros, allowing you to deliver the killing blow with your Enchanted Sword. Together, you bring down the mighty dragon through human courage and unbreakable bonds. You both survive to share the glory, proving that friendship and determination can overcome any monster.
üèÖ CROWN WITHOUT POWER
Path: Store Heart ‚Üí Fight Dragon ‚Üí Grab Crown & Flee
When the battle turns desperate, you make a hard choice. While Pyros is distracted fighting your companion, you dash for the Crown of Pyros and grab it before fleeing. You escape with the prize, but your traveler friend doesn't make it out alive. You've won the crown, but at the cost of the person who helped you reach this point. It's a hollow victory - you have the power you sought, but lost the friendship that made the journey meaningful.
üèÖ OUTWITTED FLAME
Path: Any ‚Üí Run from Dragon ‚Üí Help Traveler ‚Üí Return Together
Sometimes wisdom means knowing when to retreat. When you initially run from Pyros, your companion stumbles. Instead of abandoning them, you help them up and together devise a clever plan. You return to face the dragon, but this time with strategy instead of brute force. Working together, you lure Pyros to the edge of a cliff and trick the massive beast into falling to its doom. Your combined cunning defeats what raw strength could not. The Crown is yours through intelligence and loyalty.

üíÄ HERO'S SACRIFICE
Path: Store Heart ‚Üí Fight Dragon ‚Üí Mutual Destruction
In a final, desperate gambit, you leap at the dragon with your Enchanted Sword in a suicidal attack. Your blade finds its mark, piercing Pyros's heart, but the dragon's death throes crush you as well. Both hero and monster die together on the mountain peak. It's a pyrrhic victory - the dragon is defeated and can terrorize no more, but you pay the ultimate price. Your sacrifice becomes legend, inspiring future heroes.
üêç SNAKE'S VENOM / üó°Ô∏è SPIKE TRAP
Path: Any ‚Üí Hide in Tree ‚Üí Snake OR Spike Trap
Climbing the tree seems like the safest option, but it's actually the most dangerous. If you try to kick away a venomous snake, you miss and it bites you - the poison kills you within minutes. If you jump down to escape, you land on hidden spike traps that impale you instantly. The "safe" choice becomes a death trap. Sometimes facing danger head-on is braver than hiding.
üßô‚Äç‚ôÄÔ∏è WITCH'S CURSE
Path: Any ‚Üí Run from Dragon ‚Üí Abandon Traveler ‚Üí Find Witch
If you abandon your companion and flee alone, you discover a witch's hut. The witch offers you water (which is poisoned) or a place to sleep (which is a trap that kills you in your dreams). Either choice results in death. This ending punishes selfishness - by abandoning your friend to save yourself, you ensure your own doom. Loyalty would have saved you.
üî• BURNED ALIVE
Path: Store Heart ‚Üí Fight Dragon ‚Üí Poor Combat Choices
Without the Heart's protection, dragon fire can kill you instantly. If you hide behind a wooden box when Pyros breathes fire, you're incinerated. The flames consume both you and your hiding spot. Only quick thinking and proper cover (like rock walls) can save someone without magical protection from a dragon's burning breath.
üîÑ RESPAWN SYSTEM
All deaths lead back to Echoing Ledge checkpoint. Learn from each failure and try again!
üèîÔ∏è THE ETERNAL LOOP
After Every Death
Death is not the end in the Forest of Echoes. No matter how you die - snake venom, spike traps, dragon fire, witch's curse, or heroic sacrifice - you always awaken back at Echoing Ledge with all your progress intact. Each death teaches you something new about the challenges ahead. Eventually, armed with this knowledge, you WILL find a path to victory. The mountain remembers your struggles and gives you infinite chances to succeed.
